# Listening Practice Tips

## What matters
- capturing the main idea quickly
- noticing key details, names, and numbers
- recognizing transitions and signal phrases
- maintaining spelling accuracy in dictated content

## Common mistakes
- trying to write down every word
- missing contrast markers and summary cues
- losing track after one unfamiliar word
- weak spelling in dictation tasks

## Practice tips
- Listen for signal words like however, in contrast, and therefore.
- Write keywords and meaning, not full sentences, during note-taking.
- Practice short audio recall drills immediately after listening.
- Train with dictation to improve attention and spelling.
- Review common academic vocabulary and sound patterns.

## Test-day strategy
- Stay focused on the speaker's overall message.
- If you miss one detail, recover quickly and keep listening.
- In dictation-style tasks, reserve a moment to check spelling and grammar.

## Quick self-check
- Did I capture the main point?
- Did I record the important details?
- Did I miss the answer because of one unknown word?
