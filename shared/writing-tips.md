# Writing Practice Tips

## What matters
- staying on task
- organizing ideas clearly
- grammatical accuracy
- spelling and punctuation
- concise, controlled expression

## Common mistakes
- writing before planning
- going off-topic
- overusing long or awkward sentences
- forgetting to proofread

## Practice tips
- Spend a few seconds planning before you write.
- Use a clear structure: opening, development, and conclusion where appropriate.
- Prefer correct, controlled sentences over complicated ones.
- Practice summarizing ideas precisely in one or two sentences.
- Leave time to proofread spelling, grammar, and punctuation.

## Test-day strategy
- Keep your response focused on the prompt.
- Use transitions only when they improve clarity.
- Save time at the end for a quick review.

## Quick self-check
- Did I answer the exact task?
- Is my structure clear?
- Did I check grammar and spelling?
