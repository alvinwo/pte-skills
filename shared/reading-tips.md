# Reading Practice Tips

## What matters
- understanding the main idea quickly
- spotting paraphrases and keyword variations
- distinguishing correct answers from distractors
- managing time across passages

## Common mistakes
- reading every line too slowly
- relying only on exact keyword matches
- ignoring sentence structure and author intent
- spending too long on one difficult item

## Practice tips
- Read the question first so you know what information to find.
- Skim the passage for topic, structure, and tone before reading deeply.
- Look for synonyms and paraphrases instead of expecting exact words.
- Eliminate clearly wrong options before choosing the best answer.
- Practice breaking long sentences into smaller idea units.

## Test-day strategy
- Keep moving if one item is taking too long.
- Use evidence from the passage, not intuition alone.
- Re-check transition words such as however, therefore, and although.

## Quick self-check
- Did I identify the main idea?
- Did I verify the answer using passage evidence?
- Did I avoid getting trapped by similar wording?
