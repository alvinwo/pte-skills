# Speaking Practice Tips

## What matters
- fluency and pacing
- clear pronunciation and word stress
- logical structure
- confidence under timed pressure

## Common mistakes
- speaking too fast and losing clarity
- stopping often to self-correct
- using overly complex sentences
- forgetting a simple response structure

## Practice tips
- Speak in short, clear idea groups.
- Prioritize smooth delivery over perfect grammar.
- Use simple templates for repeated question types.
- Record yourself and review pace, clarity, and hesitation points.
- Practice every day with a timer.

## Test-day strategy
- Start confidently and keep a steady rhythm.
- If you make a small mistake, continue instead of restarting.
- Use a clear opening, two main points, and a short finish when the format allows.

## Quick self-check
- Was I easy to understand?
- Did I keep speaking without long pauses?
- Did I use a simple structure?
