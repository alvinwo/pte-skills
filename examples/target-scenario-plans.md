# Target Scenario Plan Examples

This file shows what the repo's planning guidance should look like in practice.

## Example 1: Student visa learner
### Profile
- goal: student visa English result
- time before exam: 6 weeks
- study time: 1 hour per day
- weak areas: speaking confidence, listening detail

### Recommended priorities
- RA
- RS
- WFD
- DI
- basic reading blanks

### Deprioritize for now
- reading MCQ
- listening MCQ
- minor low-yield listening tasks

### Daily plan
- 10 min vocabulary + spelling
- 10–30 min RA
- 10 min RS practice
- 10 min WFD practice
- 20 min DI or weak-skill block
- extra passive listening to RS and WFD audio whenever possible, such as walking time, commuting, or before sleep

### Weekly focus
- 4 to 5 core engine days
- 2 mixed practice days
- one official mock near the exam window if the learner needs readiness confirmation

### Main coaching message
Do not try to master every task equally. Build reliable score through the highest-yield routines first.

## Example 2: 4个7 learner with weak listening
### Profile
- goal: 4个7
- time before exam: 8 weeks
- study time: 90 minutes per day
- weak area: listening

### Recommended priorities
- WFD
- RS
- HIW
- FIB-L
- SGD
- RL

### Maintain but do not overfocus
- RA
- DI
- writing templates

### Deprioritize for now
- listening MCQ unless accuracy is extremely poor
- reading MCQ unless reading also becomes unstable

### Daily plan
- 15 min vocabulary
- 15 min RS
- 15 min WFD
- 15 min HIW / FIB-L
- 15 min RA or DI maintenance
- 15 min review log or timed mixed set

### Weekly focus
- 2 engine days
- 3 weakness days
- 1 mixed simulation day
- 1 review day
- at least one official mock before the real exam if possible

### Main coaching message
Protect the section floor. The biggest risk is not overall ability — it is one unstable score.

## Example 3: 4个8 learner with strong speaking but weak writing
### Profile
- goal: 4个8
- time before exam: 10 weeks
- study time: 2 hours per day
- strengths: speaking confidence
- weak area: writing accuracy

### Recommended priorities
- WFD
- FIB-L
- WE / SWT structure control
- spelling review
- RS for maintenance and crossover support
- reading blanks for score efficiency

### Maintain strongly
- RA
- DI
- speaking fluency routines

### Do not ignore completely
- low-yield tasks that still leak points repeatedly

### Daily plan
- 20 min vocabulary + spelling
- 20 min WFD
- 20 min RS
- 20 min reading / listening blanks
- 20 min writing structure or integrated writing task
- 20 min review and correction

### Weekly focus
- several timed mixed sets
- repeated writing cleanup
- one strong mock or section simulation each week
- official mocks strongly recommended before the real exam

### Main coaching message
At this level, the issue is not only doing more practice. It is reducing small repeated losses in spelling, structure, and writing control.
