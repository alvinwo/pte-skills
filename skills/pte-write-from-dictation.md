# PTE Write From Dictation

## Purpose
- type a sentence accurately after hearing it once
- train one of the highest-value Listening and Writing tasks

## When to use
- almost always
- especially when Listening is weak
- especially when Writing is weak

## Success looks like
- many words preserved correctly
- correct spelling
- correct small grammar signals
- logical full sentence reconstruction

## Common mistakes
- missing articles and prepositions
- missing plural or tense endings
- spelling familiar words badly under pressure
- hearing words but losing the sentence structure

## Practice method
1. listen for chunks
2. catch the sentence skeleton
3. reconstruct after the audio
4. check articles, plurals, prepositions, and tense endings
5. save failed items for review

## Special notes
- if the learner uses prediction lists, become very familiar with the current WFD pool
- use that familiarity to support practice, not replace spelling, listening, and reconstruction training

## Daily target
- 10 to 20 items
- almost every day
